Drums

My passion for drums started at a young age. The rhythmic patterns and the ability to create beats that move people have always fascinated me. Just like the precise timing in Formula 1, drumming requires perfect timing and coordination.

I've been playing drums for over 10 years, practicing various styles including rock, heavy metal and Nu Metal. The discipline required to master this instrument has taught me patience and precision that I apply to all areas of my life.

Whether it's a solo practice session or jamming with fellow musicians, I find drumming to be both exhilarating and meditative. The focus required to maintain complex rhythms clears my mind and provides a creative outlet.

  • Travis Barker

Racing

My love for racing goes beyond being a fan; it's a passion that drives me to understand the intricacies of motorsport. Formula 1, in particular, represents the perfect blend of technology, strategy, and human skill.

I actively participate in sim racing competitions, which has taught me about racing lines, throttle control, and the importance of split-second decision making. The precision and focus required mirror what F1 drivers experience on track.

Beyond virtual racing, I attend local karting events and occasionally participate in amateur racing series. The thrill of competition and the constant pursuit of improvement make racing an exhilarating hobby.

  • Daniel Ricciardo
  • Max Verstappen

Favorite F1 Circuits

  • Monaco Circuit
  • Azerbaijan Baku Circuit
  • Autódromo José Carlos Pace (Interlagos)

Archery

Archery represents the perfect balance of physical strength and mental focus. Like the precision required in F1 engineering, archery demands attention to detail and consistency in execution.

I picked up archery five years ago and have been practicing regularly ever since. The discipline has taught me about patience, focus, and the importance of form. Every shot is a new opportunity to improve.

I practice both traditional and Olympic recurve styles, finding that each offers unique challenges and rewards. The meditative quality of drawing the bow and focusing on the target provides a sense of calm that counterbalances the high-energy nature of my other hobbies.

Archery Styles

  • Traditional Longbow
  • Barebow
  • Field Archery

Equipment

  • Carbon Arrows
  • Stabilizers & Sights
  • Traditional English Longbow
